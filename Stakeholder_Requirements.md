# Stakeholder Requirements

## Primary Stakeholders
- Customer Growth Team
- Operations Team
- Executive Leadership

## Decision Context
The Customer Growth Team needs clarity on how customer demand at station locations has changed over time so they can confidently decide where to increase, reduce, or reallocate bike supply.

## Success Criteria
- Clear identification of high-, low-, and emerging-demand stations
- Alignment between growth and operations teams
- Reduced reliance on assumptions in allocation decisions
