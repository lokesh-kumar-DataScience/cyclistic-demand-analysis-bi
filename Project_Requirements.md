# Project Requirements

## Project Outcomes
1. Customer demand patterns across station locations are clearly compared over time.
2. Stations can be categorized for expansion, reduction, or reallocation.
3. Teams can prioritize actions using a shared, data-backed view of demand.

## Constraints
- Course 1 scope (planning & framing)
- No personal customer data
- Focus on location-level demand

## Measure of Success
Stakeholders can explain demand patterns and allocation priorities confidently.
